[color=#800]Keyboard controls[/color]:
Next panel: [color=blue]Space, enter[/color]
Back (previous panel): [color=blue]backspace, page_up[/color]
Toggle menu: [color=blue]Escape[/color]

Menu items can be highlighted with the arrow keys, except for some back buttons that can be activated by pressing escape.

[color=#800]Mouse controls[/color]:
The quick menu has some buttons, and you can advance the text by clicking elsewhere on screen.
